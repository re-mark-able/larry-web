What is Larry Games?

Host game nights easily with Larry Games, a group game bot with various unique games! Regular updates and improvements are being made.

AA: Acronyms Anonymous
/aa
Players use the letters provided to create a funny saying.
Adventurers
/adventurers
Collect treasures and gold over the course of 5 days and compete to bring back the most money to camp. But keep an eye out of idols and watch out for hazards!
Dice Attack
/attack
A self-playing dice game where rolls determine attack strength and defence.
Battle Boats
/boats
team, Discord version of the game of Battle Ships
Codebreaker
/codebreaker
Guess the code before the other players.
Dice Dare
/dice
Roll to get the highest score. Watch out for skulls.
Hangman
/hangman
Players guess letters to figure out the word and guess it before everyone else.
Higher or Lower
/higherlower
A random number is picked. Players guess and will be told the number is either higher or lower
Hi/Lo Dice (NEW)
/hilodice
A simple dice roll game with 5 numbered dice and 1 hi/lo dice. Players aim to get the highest score.
Larry Games
/lg
A self-playing game where random actions are picked for players.
Math Race
/mathrace
Answer basic math questions. Most points, wins.
Red Light/Green Light
/rlgl
Click the right button to stay in. 1 person is eliminated each round. Last player wins
Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock
/rpsls
From Big Bang Theory. Rock Paper Scissors with a twist.
Larry Says
/says
A Discord version of 'Simon Says'. Do what Larry says.
Sequence
/sequence
A memory game where players must remember the sequence of displayed emojis.
Shootout!
/shootout
Shoot the other player and be the last one standing.
Split or Steal
/sos
A basic game using your server XP or currency. Players choose to split the prize, or steal it all for themselves.
Two-Up
/twoup
A coin flip game. 2 coins, you pick what you think will come up.
Whodunnit?
/whodunnit
Find the killer, the weapon and the room. Based of the board game Cluedo.
Word Chain
/wordchain
A word game where players use the last letter of the previous word to start a new word.
The Curse of the Werewolf
/ww
A game of deduction. Everyone has roles either team villager or team werewolf.

All games have multiple different game modes to help change the game play!

Regular updates, new games and improvements. Need extra help? Join our support server.
